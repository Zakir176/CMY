ABOUT CMY

Our Mission

Copperbelt Muslim Youth (CMY) is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual, intellectual, and social development of Muslim youth in Zambia's Copperbelt region. Founded in 2015, we've grown into a vibrant community of over 500 active members aged 15-30.

Our Vision

To develop a generation of confident, God-conscious Muslim youth who positively impact society while preserving their Islamic identity in multicultural Zambia.
