About CMY

Copperbelt Muslim Youth (CMY) is a vibrant community organization dedicated to empowering young Muslims across the Copperbelt region of Zambia. Founded on the principles of Islamic values, education, and community service, we strive to create a supportive environment where young people can grow spiritually, intellectually, and socially.

Our mission is to provide comprehensive programs that nurture leadership skills, promote Islamic education, and encourage active participation in community development. We believe in the power of youth to create positive change while staying rooted in their faith and cultural identity.

Through various initiatives including educational workshops, community service projects, spiritual development programs, and recreational activities, CMY serves as a bridge connecting young Muslims with their heritage while preparing them for future challenges.

Our Programs

Islamic Education

Comprehensive Quran studies, Arabic language classes, and Islamic jurisprudence sessions designed to deepen understanding of our faith.

Leadership Development

Training programs focused on developing leadership skills, public speaking, and project management capabilities among young Muslims.

Community Service

Regular outreach programs including charity drives, community clean-up initiatives, and support for underprivileged families.

Sports & Recreation

Football tournaments, fitness programs, and recreational activities that promote physical wellness and team building.

Skills Development

Vocational training, entrepreneurship workshops, and career guidance to prepare youth for economic opportunities.

Marriage & Family

Counseling services, marriage preparation courses, and family support programs based on Islamic principles.
